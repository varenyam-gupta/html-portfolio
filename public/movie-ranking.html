The best movies/shows according to Varenyam

My top 3 movies/shows of all time

Attack On Titan

The GOAT. Haven't seen anything close to it. The plot, the story, the action, the animation - everything blows my mind away questioning how far a human can actually think!

Your Name

The most beautiful movie I have ever watched. The BGM and the posters always make me want to rewatch this.

Death Note

The battle between the masterminds! Not much action but the plot and IQ of characters will blow your mind away!
